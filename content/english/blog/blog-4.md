### Immediate steps to protect your property

After a hailstorm, inspect your property from the ground and document visible signs of damage such as dented gutters, displaced shingles, and granule buildup at downspouts.

Do not walk on a potentially damaged roof. Schedule a professional inspection as soon as possible.

###### Insurance-ready documentation

Take dated photos of exterior damage and interior leaks. Keep records of temporary repairs and related invoices.

###### Prevent further damage

If needed, install temporary protection to stop active leaks while permanent repairs are planned. Fast action helps avoid secondary damage to insulation, drywall, and electrical systems.
