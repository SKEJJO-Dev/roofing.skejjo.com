### Smart maintenance habits that save money

Roof longevity depends on regular maintenance, ventilation quality, and early repairs. Even high-quality systems can fail early without preventive care.

Consistent upkeep helps your roof handle severe weather and seasonal temperature swings.

###### Five practical tips

Schedule annual inspections, keep gutters clear, trim overhanging branches, address minor leaks quickly, and ensure attic ventilation is balanced.

###### Long-term value

These steps reduce emergency repair costs, improve energy efficiency, and preserve property value over time.
