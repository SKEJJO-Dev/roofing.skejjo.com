## Residential Roofing Services

Your home deserves the best protection. Our residential roofing services cover everything from new roof installations to complete replacements, using premium materials backed by manufacturer warranties.

## What We Offer

- **New Roof Installation**: Expert installation of asphalt shingles, metal roofing, and more
- **Roof Replacement**: Full tear-off and replacement with modern, energy-efficient materials
- **Roof Repair**: Fix leaks, damaged shingles, flashing issues, and more
- **Gutter Services**: Installation, cleaning, and repair of gutters and downspouts

## Materials We Use

- Architectural shingles
- Metal roofing panels
- Cedar shakes and shingles
- Slate roofing
- Flat/low-slope systems

## Our Process

1. Free on-site inspection and estimate
2. Material selection consultation
3. Professional installation by certified crew
4. Final inspection and walkthrough
5. Cleanup and debris removal
6. Documentation and warranty registration
